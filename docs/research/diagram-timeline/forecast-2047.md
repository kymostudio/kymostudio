## Highlights (projected)

### 1. The interface outlives every canvas

Twenty years after first taking #1 (2027), the assistant lineage retakes it — now *ambient*: present in every surface, summoning whichever view the moment needs rather than living in any app. The tools list has spent six decades ranking *places where diagrams happen*; the top entry is now a *presence*, which is the list's way of saying the place stopped mattering.

### 2. Static architecture enters its final listed year

The system/agent architecture diagram — on the list since the cloud wave made it inevitable in 2013 — slides to #10, fully absorbed into live views; next year it is gone. The genre the 2025 page called "the era's signature artifact" ends as a redirect, closing the loop opened in 2036.

### 3. Simulation views reach #3

Above it sit only the live view and the flowchart. The three top types now map exactly to the three tenses — what is (live), what was designed (flowchart), what could be (simulation) — a structure this timeline will keep to its end.
